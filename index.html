🔒
Absolute Guarantee

We do not operate any servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure that receives data from your device. The App runs entirely on your local device. All computations, conversions, and stored history exist only on your device and are never transmitted anywhere.

03 Local Storage on Your Device

Certain features of the App store data locally on your device to improve your experience. This local data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3.1  Conversion History

The App saves a local log of your recent number conversions using an on-device SQLite database (Android Room). This history is stored solely on your device. You have full control:

  • View your conversion history in the History tab at any time
  • Delete individual records by swiping left on any history entry
  • Clear all history permanently via Settings → Clear History
  • Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all locally stored data

3.2  App Preferences

The App stores your preferences (theme selection, step-by-step mode toggle) using Android DataStore on your local device. These preferences are personal to your device and are never transmitted or shared.

3.3  No Cloud Backup of Sensitive Data

The App's backup rules explicitly exclude the local database from Android Auto Backup and Google Cloud backup. Your conversion history is not backed up to any cloud service.

06 Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

The App is suitable for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13. Because we do not collect any personal information from any user, the App is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar laws globally.

We do not knowingly collect, maintain, or use personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in the user's jurisdiction). Since no data is collected from any user, there is no risk of inadvertent data collection from minors.

Parents and guardians may allow children to use this App with full confidence that no personal data will be collected, stored, or transmitted.

07 Data Security

The security of your information is important to us. While the App does not transmit any personal data, we have implemented the following security measures for locally stored data:

  • Local database encryption via Android's built-in storage security
  • No network endpoints or APIs that could be exploited to exfiltrate data
  • Adherence to Android security best practices in code implementation
  • No root or superuser access requested or required
  • ProGuard/R8 code obfuscation applied to release builds
  • Local storage scoped to the App's private data directory

Since no personal data is transmitted over the internet, the risk of a data breach affecting your personal information through this App is effectively zero. All locally stored data (conversion history, preferences) can be deleted by you at any time or upon uninstalling the App.

08 Your Rights and Controls

Although we do not collect personal data, we respect and support the following user rights in accordance with applicable privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, and others):

8.1  Right of Access

You have the right to know what data is stored on your device by the App. You can view your conversion history directly in the History screen of the App.

8.2  Right to Deletion

You may delete all locally stored App data at any time by:

  • Going to Settings → Clear History within the App
  • Uninstalling the App, which permanently removes all associated local data

8.3  Right to Data Portability

Since no data is transmitted to our servers, there is no remote data to export. All locally stored data is accessible via the App interface.

8.4  Right to Object / Opt-Out

Since we do not engage in any data processing, profiling, or targeted advertising, there is nothing to opt out of. The App has no tracking to disable.

09 Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no server-side data retention policy applicable. With respect to locally stored data:
